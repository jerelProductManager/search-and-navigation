About This Page

Navigation Categories are the top-level items in the green nav bar (e.g. Photography, Computers). Each has a unique CatID.


Child Category Labels are the items available when adding a Label-type featured slot. Each label can have an ID# assigned for system reference.


All changes are saved to browser storage and reflected immediately across the Nav Preview and Category Detail pages.
